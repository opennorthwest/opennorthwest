**OPEN NW** is a registered 509(a)2 public charity which strives to create an inclusive, vibrant community that supports the growth of the local Ethically Non-Monogamous culture and its people, and works to increase visibility, normalization, and acceptance of Ethical Non-monogamy in society at large.
