We have received several inquiries since our inception on whether we have any events for parents.  Currently only our annual Community Day and some of our hikes are open to children attending, and we don't have any events specifically focusing on parents.  As we continue to expand and look for new opportunities to serve our community, we're hoping to get more data from parents about what sorts of events would best serve you!

We'd love to hear from you if you are a parent, or someone who is actively interested in interacting with and supporting parents in our community.
