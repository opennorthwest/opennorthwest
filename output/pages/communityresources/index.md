What we know about other communities and resources near Portland, OR.

### Us

- [Meetup.com](https://www.meetup.com/PDX-Poly-Community/) - Find our events and RSVP.
- Discord - [Click here for an invite](https://discord.gg/fkdeDbE)!

### Other Non-Monogamy focus Communities

- **Portland Polyamory** - [Meetup.com](https://www.meetup.com/portland-polyamory-2-meetup-group/) - A bi-weekly social poly meetup.
- **Poly Movie Nights** - [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/groups/595885557260988/) - For those who love movies, and love to be comfortably surrounded by other poly people.
- **Van/Port Poly Network** \- [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/groups/vanportpolynetwork/) - Mostly Vancouver focused poly community.

### Sex Positivity

- **SPEEC** - speecportland.org - Facilitates the development of sex-positive communities in the Pacific NW. They have a [calendar of events](https://www.facebook.com/pg/SPEECPortland/events) on Facebook.
- **Sex Positive Portland** (SPP) - [www.sexpositiveportland.org](http://www.sexpositiveportland.org/) - Events centered around understanding and exploring all aspects of human sexuality, consent, relationship styles, kink, gender, and orientation.
- **Bi Brigade** - [Meetup.com](https://www.meetup.com/Bi-Brigade-Portland-OR/) - Community for those that are bisexual, pansexual, fluid, or otherwise attracted to more than one sex or gender.
