We host several regular events each month to bring folks together over shared interests & activities.  We also host a handful of special events throughout the year to provide unique opportunities for extra education, celebration, and fellowship.

[Meetup Calendar](https://www.meetup.com/PDX-Poly-Community/events/calendar/)

Exact times & locations of our events are not posted publicly.  If any of these events interest you, please review our [Conduct Guidelines](http://opennorthwest.org/conduct-guidelines/) and then join our [Meet-Up group](https://www.meetup.com/PDX-Poly-Community) to RSVP.  Hosts and [Valkyries](http://opennorthwest.org/valkyries/) at events are there to help everyone feel safe and welcome.  Please don't hesitate to reach out!

If you are looking for our **Special Event**, you have come to the right place. We cannot talk about dating on the Meetup website, however this event will be our famous [Polyamorous Speed Dating](http://opennorthwest.org/events/speed-dating/) event.
